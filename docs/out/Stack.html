Class: Stack

Stack()

Performs the immediate child Nodes of this simultaneously. The traversal times of the immediate child Nodes may differ from from each other, enabling the traversal times of those children to go out of phase and back into phase with the traversal times of this. Enables differential canons and other temporal structures, as well as contrapuntal sequences.
